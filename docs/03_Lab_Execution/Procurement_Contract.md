### 5. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

| Metric | Target | Penalty |
|--------|--------|---------|
| System Uptime | 99.5% | 5% credit if missed |

| Support Response | <2 hours | 2% credit per hour |
| Monthly Downtime Allowed | <3.6 hours | Credits applied |

### 6. RESPONSIBILITIES

**Vendor Responsibilities:**
- [ ] Provide services as specified
- [ ] Maintain service availability & performance
- [ ] Provide technical support
- [ ] Issue resolution within SLA
- [ ] Monthly performance reports

**Client Responsibilities:**
- [ ] Provide timely information/access
- [ ] Make timely payments
- [ ] Notify of issues promptly
- [ ] Cooperate in problem resolution

---

### 7. TERM & TERMINATION

**Contract Duration**: 
- Start Date: 2026-04-01
- End Date: 2026-09-30
- Initial term: 6 months

**Renewal Terms**:
- Auto-renewal: Yes / No
- Renewal notice: 30 days before expiration

**Termination Rights**:
- Termination for convenience: 30 days notice + reasonable cost
- Termination for cause: Immediate if material breach

**Early Termination Clause**:
- If vendor fails to meet SLA for 2 consecutive months
- Client can terminate without penalty

### 8. CONFIDENTIALITY & IP RIGHTS

**Intellectual Property:**

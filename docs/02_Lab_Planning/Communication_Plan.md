# Communication Plan - Lab 2

## 📢 Communication Strategy

### Communication Objectives
1. Keep all stakeholders informed of project status
2. Ensure timely escalation of risks and issues
3. Maintain team alignment and coordination
4. Document decisions and action items
5. Manage expectations and stakeholder satisfaction

---

## 👥 Stakeholder Communication Matrix

| Stakeholder | Type | Frequency | Medium | Owner | Content |
|-------------|------|-----------|--------|-------|---------|
| Executive Sponsor | External | Monthly | Email + Meeting | PM | Status, risks, decisions |
| Client/Product Owner | External | Weekly | Meeting + Email | PM | Progress, deliverables, changes |
| Project Team | Internal | Daily | Stand-up | PM | Tasks, blockers, coordination |

## 📅 Communication Schedule

### Daily Communications

#### 1. Daily Standup Meeting
- **Time**: 9:30 AM (15 minutes)
- **Participants**: All team members
- **Format**: Virtual (Zoom/Teams)
- **Content**:
  - What did I complete yesterday?
  - What will I complete today?
  - What blockers do I have?
- **Owner**: PM or Scrum Master

#### 2. Slack/Teams Channel
- **Channels**: #general, #dev-backend, #dev-frontend, #qa-testing
- **Purpose**: Quick questions, updates, coordination
- **Response Time**: Within 2 hours

---

### Weekly Communications

#### 1. Project Status Meeting
- **Day**: Monday, 10:00 AM
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Participants**: PM, Tech Lead, Backend Lead, Frontend Lead, QA Lead
- **Agenda**:
  - Progress update (% complete by work package)
  - Risks & issues
  - Schedule impact
  - Budget burn rate
  - Action items from last week

#### 2. Development Team Sync
- **Day**: Wednesday, 2:00 PM
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Participants**: Backend Team, Frontend Team
- **Agenda**:
  - Integration points
  - Technical decisions
  - Dependency coordination

#### 3. QA Team Meeting
- **Day**: Thursday, 10:00 AM
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Participants**: QA Lead, QA Engineers
- **Agenda**:
  - Test status
  - Defect trending
  - Testing blockers

#### 4. Stakeholder Update
- **Day**: Friday, 3:00 PM
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Participants**: PM, Client/Sponsor, Tech Lead
- **Agenda**:
  - Week summary
  - Deliverables completed
  - Next week preview
  - Open issues

---

### Monthly Communications

#### 1. Project Steering Committee Meeting
- **Frequency**: Last Friday of each month
- **Duration**: 1.5 hours
- **Participants**: Executive Sponsor, Client, PM, Tech Lead
- **Agenda**:
  - Overall project status (RAG status)
  - Budget performance
  - Risk assessment
  - Milestone achievements
  - Decision items

#### 2. Project Report
- **Format**: Email/Dashboard
- **Content**:
  - Overall status (RAG)
  - Progress metrics (% complete)
  - Budget vs. Actual
  - Risk & Issue summary
  - Milestone forecast
  - Decisions & approvals
